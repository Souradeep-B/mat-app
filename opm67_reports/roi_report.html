Step 4 — Who Moved Up? (TEST vs CONTROL Bucket Migration)
📝 How to read this table — simpler than it looks
Each ROW = the bucket a user was in when the email was sent (March 3).
Each COLUMN = the bucket that same user was in 15 days later (March 18).
Yellow diagonal cells = user stayed in the same bucket (no change).
Green cells = user moved UP to a higher bucket — they earned more of their reward! 🎉

Real example from this data: For example: 87 users who were in the 0% bucket at send time had moved up to 1-25% by 15 days later — they earned more of their reward after the email.

To find what % of a bucket moved up: Add all the green numbers in that row ÷ the row total (shown in the row label).
Example: if the “1–25%” row has 3,000 users and the green cells add up to 90 → 90 ÷ 3,000 = 3% of that bucket moved up.
